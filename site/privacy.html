Privacy Policy

Warranty Box is an offline-first app. All data you enter is stored locally on your device in the app's Application Support directory and is never transmitted to any server operated by us or any third party.

We do not collect, sell, or share any personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or network tracking of any kind.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment information.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is removed from your device.
